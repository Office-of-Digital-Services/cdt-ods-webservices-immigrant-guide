Legal Help & Citizenship

Legal Help

California funds programs at community based organizations that help immigrants and refugees who live in California. The programs offer many types of free legal services, as well as information about other services that support immigrants. These organizations can teach you about your rights and some can also give legal advice on how to change your immigration status.

Become a U.S. Citizen

If you are a permanent resident with a Green Card, you may qualify to become U.S. citizen through "naturalization." There are many benefits to U.S. citizenship. U.S. citizens have the right to vote and run for public office. They can also apply for a U.S. passport. We encourage you to find out if you qualify to become a U.S. citizen and learn more about what you need to do to apply.

Additional Legal Help & Citizenship Resources

  • Learn About Getting a Green Card

    The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services agency offers information for becoming a Permanent Resident of the United States.

  • Study for the U.S. Citizenship Test

    The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services agency offers information about taking the required tests for becoming a naturalized U.S. citizen.

  • Translated Citizenship Questions

    The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services agency offers translations of the questions asked during a naturalization interview.

  • Free USCIS Information Meetings

    The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services agency offers free informational meetings. These meetings are open to the public and include information on the naturalization process.
