Public Benefits

Many benefits are available to Californians, some regardless of immigration status. These include benefits like food & nutrition, healthcare, housing and small business assistance.

You are not a public charge if you collect any of these benefits:

  • Food & nutrition benefits, including:
    • Free meals for children through school
    • GetCalFresh.org benefits
    • Women, Infants & Children (WIC) benefits
  • Unemployment insurance
  • Paid family or sick leave
  • Workers’ compensation
  • Disability insurance
  • And many more

Additional Public Benefits

  • BenefitsCal

    BenefitsCal is a new website that will replace C4Yourself, YourBenefitsNow, and MyBenefitsCalWIN, to provide one unified experience for all Californians, everywhere.

  • California Small Business COVID-19 Relief Grant

    All Californians can apply for financial help through IBank.

  • Get CalFresh

    Get money for food. Apply for food stamps in 10 minutes.

  • Golden State Stimulus

    Qualified families and individuals will receive this payment after filing their 2020 tax return.

  • US Small Business Administration programs

    Non-US citizens with documentation of their legal status can receive financial help, including Paycheck Protection Plan loans and Economic Injury Disaster Loans.

  • Housing for the Harvest

    This program offers temporary hotel housing to agricultural and food processing workers, including farm workers, who need to isolate due to COVID-19. This program gives exposed agricultural workers a place to stay and meals so they can protect their loved ones until they recover.

  • Housing Is Key

    Renters, homeowners, and small landlords can get relief if COVID-19 or quarantine impacts your ability to pay all or part of your rent or mortgage.

  • Local Department of Social Services Office Lookup

    Lookup the office locations for your county Department of Social Services.

  • Water shut-offs

    The Water Board has restricted water service shut offs during the COVID-19 crisis. Fill out this form to report any water shut off or reconnection issue.
