Find Jobs & Training

California has services to help people find jobs. Community based organizations, adult schools, and community colleges have programs and services that can help you get job training and increase your job skills. These organizations have events like job hiring fairs, and give classes in job training and career technical education.

Additional Jobs and Training Resources

  • California's Workforce Development Boards

    Local Workforce Development Boards offer services for local residents and businesses. Their services include One-Stop Career Centers, where you can find workforce, education, and business services.

  • La Cooperativa

    La Cooperativa offers education and employment services in rural areas through its member organizations. Their programs help farmworkers with job readiness and training programs. La Cooperativa and its members implement the National Farmworker Jobs Program in California.

  • Migrant and Seasonal Farmworker Outreach Program

    The Migrant and Seasonal Farmworker Outreach Program has recruitment services for migrant seasonal farmworker job seekers including job search assistance, CalJOBS SM navigation information, and registration and support in accessing other Employment Development Department programs.
